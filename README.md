# Patient Waiting Time Analysis – A Patient Perspective

## About the Project

Have you ever visited a hospital and felt frustrated because of long waiting times? Waiting is one of the most common concerns patients face during healthcare visits. This project was developed to understand where patients spend the most time waiting, what factors contribute to delays, and how these delays affect their overall experience.

Using Power BI, I analyzed survey data collected from patients to identify patterns in waiting times and evaluate patient satisfaction. The goal was not only to study the problem but also to provide practical recommendations that healthcare organizations can use to improve their services.

## Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project were:

* To understand patient experiences related to waiting times.
* To identify the stages where maximum delays occur.
* To examine factors influencing waiting time, such as crowding and appointment methods.
* To analyze the impact of waiting time on patient satisfaction.
* To suggest improvements that can enhance healthcare service quality.

## Key Insights

The analysis revealed several interesting findings:

* Doctor consultation was the stage where patients experienced the longest waiting time.
* Patients without prior appointments generally waited longer than those with scheduled appointments.
* High levels of crowding significantly increased waiting times.
* Poor queue management negatively affected patient experiences.
* Longer waiting times were strongly associated with lower satisfaction levels.
* Digital technologies helped reduce delays but still have room for improvement.

## Recommendations

Based on the findings, the following recommendations were suggested:

1. Improve appointment scheduling systems.
2. Introduce better queue management practices.
3. Increase staff availability during peak hours.
4. Encourage digital registration and check-in systems.
5. Monitor waiting times using real-time dashboards.
6. Collect regular patient feedback for continuous improvement.
